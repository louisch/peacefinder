Dillon's Coffee Waterstones

Dillon's Coffee Waterstones, Ground Floor, Waterstones, 82 Gower St London WC1E 6EQ
2 minute walk from UCL Main Quad
Right next to the main quad of UCL, on the ground floor of Waterstones, this coffee shop offers a great atmosphere for students to work in with free Wifi and plug sockets dotted around. The prices are a little on the higher end, but the 10% student discount allows a little leeway. The freshly baked cakes and pastries are not to be missed, not to mention the build-your-own-pancakes.
3.5/5
